Frequently Asked Questions

What is this?
Honestly, it started out as a random project because I was bored lol. But basically, it's a quick tool that lets you slap the iconic Nintendo Switch (or Switch 2!) logo animation right onto the front of any video clip. It runs completely inside your browser so you don't have to open up a massive video editor just to make a clean edit or meme.
Are my videos being uploaded anywhere?
Nope! Everything happens completely inside your browser using WebAssembly. Your video clips never leave your computer, making it 100% private.
Why does the first video take a moment to start?
The very first time you click "Merge", the page has to boot up the video processing engine inside a separate background worker thread. Once that engine is warmed up and sitting in memory, any other videos you process in the same session will start moving immediately.
