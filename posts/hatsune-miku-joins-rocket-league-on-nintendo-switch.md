Exciting news for gaming and music fans alike! Starting today, **Rocket League** players on Nintendo Switch can dive into the **Hatsune Miku x Rocket League** event, running through February 11. By completing in-game challenges, players can unlock exclusive Hatsune Miku-themed rewards, including Toppers, Decals, and the Melt Player Anthem, featuring her signature synthesized vocals.

Hatsune Miku, the world's biggest virtual superstar, brings her unique style to the high-octane soccer-meets-driving game, offering fans a blend of music and gaming like never before. Don't miss out on this limited-time collaboration to showcase your love for Miku on the field!
