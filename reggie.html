📌 FAQ

  • Is Reggie available to the public? Nope—invite-only for now.
  • Will Reggie ever be public? Honestly? I don’t even know. Maybe someday. Maybe never. It’s like asking if Waluigi will ever get his own game—hope exists, but we live in chaos.
  • How do I become a tester? You don’t. It’s entirely up to Cody who gets in—there’s no form, request process, or secret handshake. 😎
  • Can I suggest features? Absolutely. Cody’s always open to cool ideas.
  • Why is it called Reggie? Because the legend himself, Reggie Fils-Aimé, is a king. Simple as that.
  • Does Reggie store any data? Nope. It doesn’t log messages or track users. Just here to vibe.

  • Why are some commands under Doug? Because Kite limits Reggie to 25 commands. Reggie’s younger brother Doug Bowser holds the extras. How to use him? That’s for you to figure out.
