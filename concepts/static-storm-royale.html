STATIC STORM ROYALE

Jackson, Georgia | 1980s Retro-Future Hybrid

Concept Visualization

Atmospheric rendering of the 1980s-2026 crossover.

The Core Experience

In this tactical Battle Royale, the oppressive Georgia weather is the primary catalyst for combat. As the humidity peaks and scattered thunderstorms roll in, the "Static Storm" triggers temporal rifts that pull Joseph and his rivals from 1980s Jackson into the high-stakes 2026 arena.

Environmental Mechanics

Players must use the hazy visibility to ambush opponents. As lightning strikes, the environment shifts, opening "Past-Portals" that allow players to retreat to the 80s for superior cover or supplies. Heat-exhaustion and lightning strikes are as lethal as enemy gunfire.

Discarded Concepts

Retro-Rift Jackson

Joseph is a man out of time, ripped from the neon-soaked streets of 1980s Jackson, Georgia, and thrust into a decaying 2026 cyberpunk wasteland. Players manage a "Nostalgia Meter" to maintain effective 1980s weaponry.

The Cody Clause

This concept introduces an unbeatable antagonist, Cody, as a permanent gameplay hurdle. Joseph is trapped in a temporal loop where success is about outlasting other contestants while the unstoppable Cody systematically clears the map.
