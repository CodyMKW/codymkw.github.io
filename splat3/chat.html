Splatoon 3 Chatroom

The chat is best viewed on a larger screen. For a better chat experience on mobile, we recommend using the Xat mobile app. Download it from Android or iOS.

Once installed use the search bar in the app and type "Splatoon3" the Splatoon 3 chatroom will come up.

Chatroom Rules:

  • Be cool to each other. This means treating others with respect, even if you disagree with them. No trolling, flaming, or hate speech.
  • Stay on topic. This is a chatroom for Splatoon 3, so let's keep our discussions related to the game. If you want to talk about something else, find a new chatroom.
  • Don't spam or flood. One message at a time, please. If you have something important to say, say it once.
  • Don't advertise your stuff. This isn't a marketplace. If you want to promote your social media accounts, website, or product, do it elsewhere.
  • Don't share personal info. This includes your real name, address, phone number, or any other information that could be used to identify you. If you want to share personal information, do it with people you trust.
  • Report any problems to the mods. If you see someone breaking the rules, report it to the owner or moderators. They're here to help keep the chatroom a fun and welcoming place for everyone.
