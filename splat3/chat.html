    Chat Rules

  1. Be cool to each other. This means treating others with respect, even if you disagree with them. No trolling, flaming, or hate speech.
  2. Stay on topic. This is a chatroom for Splatoon 3, so let's keep our discussions related to the game. If you want to talk about something else, find a new chatroom.
  3. Don't spam or flood. 0ne message at a time, please. If you have something important to say, say it 0nce.
  4. Don't advertise your stuff. This isn't a marketplace. If you want to promote your social media accounts, website, or product, do it elsewhere.
  5. Don't share personal info. This includes your real name, address, phone number, or any other information that could be used to identify you. If you want to share personal information, do it with people you trust.
  6. Report any problems to the mods. If you see someone breaking the rules, report it to the owner or moderators. They're here to help keep the chatroom a fun and welcoming place for everyone.

By following these rules, you can help to keep the Splatoon 3 chatroom a fun and welcoming place for everyone.
