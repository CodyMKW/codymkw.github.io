Frequently Asked Questions

What is this?
Another random thing made out of pure boredom lol. It lets you generate customized 3-panel festival banners to settle incredibly low-stakes arguments with friends, complete with aggressive diagonal division slashes.
How does the rendering work?
Instead of sending your text data to a backend server, it uses the local HTML5 Canvas coordinate context matrix directly inside your browser window. This compiles a clean, lossless PNG asset on the fly.
